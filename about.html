Who We Are

A New Kind of
Wellness Destination

GoWellthy was founded by Leonet from a deep personal passion — a belief that every person deserves access to technologies that help their body heal, recover, and thrive naturally.

Located within a trusted healthcare environment in the Helderberg region, GoWellthy is not a typical wellness studio. It is a space where clinical credibility meets the warmth and comfort of home — where every client feels genuinely welcomed, seen, and cared for.

We believe that true wellbeing starts at a cellular level. By supporting the body's natural energy production and regenerative capabilities, we help individuals feel stronger, healthier, and more empowered to live life to the fullest.

Brand Purpose

To help people live healthier, more vibrant lives by providing access to advanced wellness technologies that support the body's natural ability to heal, recover, and thrive.

Brand Mission

To empower people to optimise their health, recovery, and longevity through innovative wellness therapies that enhance cellular energy, support regeneration, and promote overall wellbeing.

Brand Vision

To become a leading cellular wellness destination where innovation, science, and holistic health unite to help people age beautifully, recover naturally, and enjoy a higher quality of life.

Our Promise

Every Experience Is Designed to Help You
Feel Better

At GoWellthy, every experience is designed to help you feel better, move better, recover better, and live better. We combine advanced wellness technology with compassionate care to support your journey towards greater vitality, longevity, and wellbeing.

Whether your goal is improved recovery, enhanced vitality, healthy ageing, or simply feeling your best every day — we are committed to providing safe, effective, and innovative wellness experiences that help you live well, stay healthy, and thrive.
