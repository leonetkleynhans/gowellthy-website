Flagship Treatment

Photobiomodulation Panel

The Photobiomodulation Panel is an advanced full-body light therapy system that delivers precisely calibrated red and near-infrared light wavelengths to your entire body simultaneously — supporting cellular energy production, recovery, and regeneration at their source.

Unlike surface-level treatments, photobiomodulation works at a mitochondrial level. Light energy is absorbed by the cells, stimulating ATP (adenosine triphosphate) production — the body's primary cellular fuel — resulting in measurable improvements across recovery, inflammation, skin health, and overall vitality.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Is photobiomodulation therapy safe?
+
Yes, completely. Photobiomodulation uses non-thermal, non-UV light wavelengths that are clinically studied and considered safe for most people. There is no radiation, no heat damage, and no known side effects. It is a non-invasive, painless, and relaxing experience.
How many sessions will I need to see results?
+
Most clients notice subtle improvements after 2–3 sessions, with more significant results appearing after 6–10 sessions. For chronic conditions or skin goals, we recommend consistent sessions over 8–12 weeks. Ongoing maintenance sessions help sustain the benefits.
What should I wear during a session?
+
To maximise light absorption, we recommend wearing minimal clothing. Light does not penetrate well through fabric, so the more skin exposed, the more effective your session will be. We provide privacy and a comfortable, discreet environment.
Are there any contraindications?
+
Photobiomodulation is not recommended for individuals who are pregnant, have active cancer, are photosensitive, or are taking photosensitising medications. Please consult your healthcare provider if you have any concerns. Our team will conduct a brief health screening before your first session.
How long is each session?
+
Standard sessions are 20 minutes. Extended sessions (30 minutes) are available and recommended for clients with specific recovery or therapeutic goals. Allow approximately 30–40 minutes in total including preparation and a post-session check-in.
Can I combine light therapy with other treatments?
+
Absolutely. Photobiomodulation pairs exceptionally well with physiotherapy, massage, strength training recovery, and aesthetic treatments. Many clients use it as a complement to their existing health routines to amplify results.
